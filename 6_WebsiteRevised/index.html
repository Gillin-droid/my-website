Adopsyon ay Isang Opsyon

Our outreach program focuses on providing food and water for dogs and cats in need. To achieve this, we have partnered with We Care Animal Shelter, located at 44 Joseph Street, Brgy. Concepcion Uno. At the shelter, we will feed the animals and provide them with love. Additionally, we plan to create a Facebook page to promote these pets and help them find loving homes.
Volunteer Recruitment Drive

Date: February 7, 14 Location: Room 304, JHS Building In this event, we find volunteers from our classroom to manage and sustain the program we have set. We then provide them with a short training session on their roles and responsibilities.

Shelter Visit and Feeding

Date: February 21 Location: We Care Animal Shelter In this event, our classroom goes to We Care Animal Shelter and provide food (dry and need) and water for the dogs and cats in need. While here, we also provide them with love and care.
