Our Program

Every Paw Deserves a Loving Home

This is the only program that we have set. In this program, we will establish a dedicated facebook page that will advertise these dogs and cats to help them find loving homes. We will find willing, responsible, and capable volunteers in our classroom that will manage this page.

Impact

Our Project's Goal and Impact is to give pets a second chance at living a meaningful and wonderful life and give them enjoyment in the time they still have. Hopefully our team can provide these pets with the materials they need for survival.
